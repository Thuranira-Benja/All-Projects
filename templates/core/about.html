Who We Are

Meru Young Professionals (MYP) is a non-political, non-profit youth-led organization that brings together professionals from the Mt. Kenya region and beyond. We are a dynamic community of ambitious young leaders dedicated to driving positive change in our communities.

Founded in 2024, MYP brings together talented individuals from various fields to collaborate, learn, and grow together while making a meaningful impact in our community.

Our Story

MYP was born from a vision to create a platform where young professionals could connect, collaborate, and contribute to the development of the Mt. Kenya region and beyond.

What started as a small group of passionate individuals has grown into a vibrant community of over 1,200 members, all committed to excellence, innovation, and service.
