Want to know about us ?

This is BookApp, an online library system founded by Benjamin Thuranira, founder & coordinator at Meru National Polytechnic. The main aim of BookApp is to connect people who have home-based personal libraries into one virtual library, enabling them to share different books from various regions across the globe. Users can upload books they want to share and also access shared books from other users.
